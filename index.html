A public Minecraft Server made to help raise money for Charity Water

About Us

We are a team of high school students who are motivated to bring joy to others while raising money for charity through Minecraft. Water is a human right, so by raising awareness and supporting safe water, we help those in need. Your donation can help give safe water in communities that need it, also supporting the idea that clean water should be prioritized for all
